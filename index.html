    I'm a student at GSU majoring in Computer Science. I learned about coding bootcamps while completing an assignment, researching possible careers. I was inspired how a Coding Bootcamp had advanced the career of the woman in the article I read. Afterwards I researched coding bootcamps in Atlanta and discovered GT's coding bootcamp. I have been interested in coding and am excited about the potential opportunites this could provide.

    I'm in week 3 of the 24 week coding bootcamp program. I'm also plan on completing my Associate degree in Computer Science by Spring 2018 at the latest but possibly as early as Fall 2017. My goals are to start working as a Full stack, Front-end, or Back-end developer after completion of GT's Coding Bootcamp.
